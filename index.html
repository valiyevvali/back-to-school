Polygraf AI Content Detector will tell you whether content was artificially generated or enhanced, and which AI model created it.

Detects the original source of the content, providing URLs and exact sentence or phrase matches from the source.

The text is checked based on six things: how complex it is, how easy it is to read, the variety of words used, how predictable it is, the average sentence length, and the average word length. Higher scores in complexity and readability usually mean it's human-written, while longer words, sentences, and more predictable patterns often point to AI.
